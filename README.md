# publicsector


Strategic Design In Practice
A practical introduction to applying User-Centred Design (UCD), Government Digital Service (GDS) principles, Agile delivery and Strategic Design within public sector and service transformation programmes. 
Purpose
This deck was developed to provide a concise, practitioner-focused overview of:

User-Centred Design (UCD)
Government Digital Service (GDS) principles
Agile delivery approaches
Service design and service maturity
Strategic design practices
Design assurance and governance
Inclusive and evidence-led service improvement
Responsible use of AI within public services

The material is intended as a practical reference rather than a comprehensive methodology. 

Intended Audience

Suitable for:

Service designers
User researchers
Product managers
Programme managers
Transformation leads
Policy professionals
Public sector digital teams
Commissioners and senior leaders
Organisations adopting design-led approaches to service improvement

Key Themes
The deck explores how organisations can:

Start with user needs.
Design inclusively.
Use evidence to inform decisions.
Build measurable and improvable services.
Connect strategy to delivery.
Work iteratively using Agile approaches.
Adopt GDS principles and service standards.
Establish effective design governance and assurance.
Improve service maturity over time.
